# HeyGen Video Plan — Community Welcome / Thank-You

**Avatar:** Custom avatar clone (or Photo Avatar) of Nelson in the Taylormade Creative varsity jacket
**Workflow:** HeyGen + ElevenLabs (recommended — voice your own clone for realism)
**Duration:** ~55 seconds
**Background:** Plain navy or your studio — keep it simple
**Aspect:** 9:16 for Reels/TikTok/Stories, 16:9 for the website/welcome email
**Estimated cost:** ~1 min of credits (+ ElevenLabs if used)

---

## Script

**HOOK (0–3s):**
"Hey — it's Nelson. If you're seeing this, you just joined the Taylormade Academy community… and I had to say thank you."

**BODY:**
"Real talk — most people scroll right past this stuff and never start."
[PAUSE]
"You didn't. That already puts you ahead of the pack."
[PAUSE]
"This is where creators like you learn the craft — AI, design, photography, video — and actually build something real with it. No gatekeeping. No fluff. Just people figuring it out together, and me handing you everything I know."
[PAUSE]
"So here's all I ask: don't just lurk. Jump in the feed and introduce yourself. Tell us what you're working on, or what you're trying to learn. The people who win in here are the ones who show up."

**CTA (last 5s):**
"Grab the free AI Playbook while you're here, drop a post and say hey, and let's build. I'll see you inside."

---

## Production Notes
- **Realism:** keep gestures minimal (a nod, an open-hand beat) — over-animation reads fake. Use a custom clone over a stock avatar; pair with an ElevenLabs voice clone of your own voice for the most natural result.
- **Pacing:** the `[PAUSE]` markers are natural breath breaks — they keep the delivery from feeling rushed/robotic.
- **Pronunciation:** "Taylormade Academy" — if the TTS clips it, type "Taylor-made Academy" to space it out.
- **Post:** cut in 1–2 quick b-roll shots (the app, the ebook cover, a design/photo clip) over the BODY to break up continuous avatar footage and lift retention. Add captions — most people watch muted.
- **Where to use it:** pin it as the first post in the community + the Facebook group, and drop it in the welcome email so every new member gets greeted by you.
