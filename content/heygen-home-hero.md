# HeyGen Video Plan — Home-Screen Hero (cold visitor)

**Audience:** Cold visitor on academy.taylormadecreative.net who has NOT signed up yet. Goal = land the **learn → create → EARN** promise in one breath (earn is the hook) and drive "Join Free."
**Honesty guardrail:** sell the income angle aspirationally — NO dollar figures, no "guaranteed," no fake testimonials. "Turn your skills into income" / "get paid for it," not "make $10k a month."
**Avatar:** Custom avatar clone (or Photo Avatar) of Nelson in the Taylormade Creative varsity jacket — same look as the hero photo so the page feels cohesive
**Workflow:** HeyGen + ElevenLabs (voice your own clone for realism)
**Duration:** ~25 seconds (cold visitors bounce — punchy, casual, creator-voice)
**Background:** Plain navy or the studio; or shoot it to drop cleanly onto the blue hero blob
**Aspect:** 16:9 for the site hero; export a 9:16 cutdown for Reels/TikTok/Stories ads
**Estimated cost:** ~1 min of credits (+ ElevenLabs if used)

---

## Script

**HOOK (0–4s):**
"Yo, what's up — I'm Nelson Taylor, aka Taylormade Creative online. Welcome to Taylormade Academy."

**BODY:**
"Design, photo, video, AI — fourteen years of it, all in one spot. You learn the craft, create real work, and turn it into income. That's the whole point."
[PAUSE]
"And it starts free — join the community and grab the free AI Playbook."

**CTA (last 4s):**
"Learn it. Create it. Earn from it. Hit Join Free — I'll see you inside."

## Production Notes
- **Autoplay reality:** a home-hero video almost always autoplays MUTED, so captions are non-negotiable and the FIRST FRAME must read on its own (Nelson + a bold on-frame line — use **"Learn. Create. Earn."** as the kinetic-text spine: the three words hit on-screen as he says each one). Add a visible play/unmute affordance.
- **Realism:** custom clone over a stock avatar; ElevenLabs voice clone of your own voice. Minimal gestures (a nod, one open-hand beat) — over-animation reads fake.
- **Pacing:** the `[PAUSE]` markers are breath breaks so it doesn't feel rushed. Pronunciation: type "Taylor-made Academy" if the TTS clips it.
- **B-roll:** cut 2–3 quick shots over the BODY — a design, a photo, a video clip, the app/community feed — to show the four crafts and lift retention. End on the "Join Free" button.
- **Length discipline:** if it runs long, the BODY is where to trim — keep the hook and the CTA intact.
- **Where to use it:** the home hero (16:9), plus the 9:16 cutdown as a paid/organic ad pointing at academy.taylormadecreative.net.
